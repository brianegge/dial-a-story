# Dial-a-Story - Bedtime Stories Hotline 📖

Transform your Home Assistant into a magical bedtime stories hotline!

## What is this?

Give your toddler (ages 2-5) a real phone number they can call to hear fresh, AI-generated bedtime stories read aloud. Perfect for establishing bedtime routines and giving kids independence.

## Quick Facts

- 📞 Works with any phone (landline, mobile, etc.)
- 🤖 AI generates unique stories each call
- 🎙️ Natural text-to-speech narration
- 💰 Costs only $3-5/month
- 🔒 Privacy-first (runs in your HA)
- 📊 Built-in call logging

## What You Need

1. **Telnyx account** (free sign-up)
2. **Phone number** from Telnyx ($1/month)
3. **Home Assistant** with HTTPS access (Nabu Casa works!)
4. **(Optional)** OpenAI API key for better stories

## Setup Time

**15-20 minutes** from install to first call!

## How It Works

Kid calls → HA answers → Tells story → Asks if they want another → Says goodnight!

## Features

✅ Multiple story themes (dinosaurs, trains, bunnies, etc.)  
✅ Adjustable story length (short/medium/long)  
✅ Male or female voice options  
✅ Max 3 stories per call (cost control)  
✅ Works even if internet/OpenAI is down (3 backup stories)  
✅ Full call logging and tracking  

## Cost Breakdown

- Phone number: $1/month
- Inbound calls: ~$1/month (5 calls/day @ 3min each)
- TTS: ~$1.50/month
- OpenAI (optional): ~$1.50/month

**Total: $3-5/month** 💸

Compare to: Spotify Kids ($10/mo), Audiobook apps ($15+/mo)

## Perfect For

- Bedtime routine establishment
- Teaching phone skills safely
- Reducing screen time before bed
- Kids who resist parent-read stories
- Grandparents who live far away (can add their voice later!)
